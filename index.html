Step 3 — Distribution
Platform-Specific
Adaptations
📸
Instagram Reels
Short punchy hook. First 3 seconds critical. Emoji-heavy captions. Ends with "save this" or "tag a friend" CTA. Aesthetic visuals. Target: 18–28 year olds with high energy tone.
👍
Facebook Feed
Longer captions perform better. Emotional storytelling over hype. Softer CTAs ("learn more", "visit us"). Targets 25–40 age group. Cozy, relatable tone works best for this audience.
▶️
YouTube Shorts
Outcome-specific hooks ("I actually finished all my work"). Subtitles essential. No emojis in opening line. Productivity and results angle resonates strongly with student audience.
